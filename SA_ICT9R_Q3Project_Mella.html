5 Tips you should know to prevent fire

Be aware!

Take time to understand these tips about how to prevent fires to create a safer home for you and your family.

Tips
> Make sure to properly contain active wires and cables.
> Store flammable materials in safe ares where children cannot access it.
> Be cautious when fire is being used (cooking, smoking and etc.)
> Extinguish fire and dispose properly after use.
> Electrical installations should checked from time to time.
